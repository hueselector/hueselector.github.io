Artist: Vanilla
Genre: Electronic, Hip-Hop
Release Date: 20 Sep 2015
Record Label: Self-released
About

A sample-heavy hip-hop, soul, electronic album with enough groove to carry your whole weekend. The whole project was inspired when Hugo Harrison, aka Vanilla, took a trip to Japan and had Samurai Champloo and Nujabes on heavy rotation, and you can hear it. Track 2, Dreamcatcher, actually inspired my track Inamuragasaki. Easily top 50 for me..
